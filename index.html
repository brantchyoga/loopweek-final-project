PingPong

Entering a number and pushing submit will create a list counting up to your number

Conditions:

  1. Any number divisible by 15 will be replaced with "pingpong"
  2. Any number divisible by 5 will be replaced with "pong"
  3. Any number divisible by 3 will be replaced with "ping"
